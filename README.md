# Topology · Interactive

**Topological spaces, continuity, compactness, and connectedness**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 4 · Graduate

## What You'll Learn

- Topological spaces and continuous functions
- Connectedness and compactness
- Countability and separation axioms
- The Tychonoff theorem
- Metrization theorems
- The fundamental group and covering spaces

## References

- James R. Munkres, *Topology* (2nd ed., Pearson)
- John M. Lee, *Introduction to Topological Manifolds* (2nd ed., GTM 202, Springer)
- Allen Hatcher, *Algebraic Topology* (Cambridge, introductory chapters)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
